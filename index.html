Important Information

Client policies

Please read our policies carefully before booking. These are in place to ensure a smooth, respectful, and enjoyable experience for everyone.

📅
Bookings & Appointments

All appointments must be booked in advance. Please arrive on time. A 20–30 minute late arrival may result in a shortened service or cancellation.

💳
Deposits & Payments

A non-refundable deposit (30–50%) is required to secure your booking. Deposits go toward your final total. Final payment is due immediately after your appointment. We accept EFT, cash, or mobile payment.

⏰
Cancellations & Rescheduling

24 hours' notice required for cancellations or changes. Late cancellations result in loss of deposit. One reschedule is allowed if done within the correct timeframe.

🕐
Late Arrivals

Arriving 20–30 minutes late may result in cancellation. Service time may be reduced to avoid delays for other clients.

🏥
Hygiene & Safety

All tools are thoroughly sanitized between clients. No services will be performed on infected or damaged nails. Brush Tip Studio reserves the right to refuse service if necessary.

👧
Guests

No extra guests unless arranged beforehand. Our space is designed to be calm and focused.

🏠
Home Salon Notice

Brush Tip Studio is a home-based salon. Pets may be present. Please inform me of any allergies beforehand.

🔁
Repairs & Fixes

Free fixes within 3 days for workmanship-related issues only. No free repairs for client-caused damage.

📸
Social Media

Nail photos/videos may be taken for content. Please let me know if you prefer not to be posted.

💬
Communication

Replies will be during business hours. Please allow time for responses.
