About Us

Crafted with
passion.

Brush Tip Studio is a home-based nail business dedicated to creating beautiful, high-quality nail designs in a comfortable and personal setting. Our mission is to help every client feel confident and polished — through customized nail art, meticulous attention to detail, and a truly relaxing experience.

As a newly established studio, we are focused on building strong relationships, maintaining excellent hygiene standards, and continuously improving our skills and techniques. We are growing alongside our community — and grateful for every client who trusts us with their nails.

Important Information

Client policies

Please read our policies carefully before booking. These are in place to ensure a smooth, respectful, and enjoyable experience for everyone.

📅
Bookings & Appointments

All appointments must be booked in advance. Please arrive on time. A 20–30 minute late arrival may result in a shortened service or cancellation at our discretion.

💳
Deposits & Payments

A non-refundable deposit of 30–50% is required to secure your booking. Deposits go toward your final total. Full payment is due immediately after your appointment. We accept EFT, cash, or mobile payment.

🔄
Cancellations & Rescheduling

24 hours' notice is required for any cancellations or changes. Late cancellations result in loss of deposit. One reschedule is allowed if done within the correct timeframe.

⏰
Late Arrivals

Arriving 20–30 minutes late may result in cancellation of your appointment. Service time may be reduced to avoid delays for other clients. Your deposit may be forfeited.

🏥
Hygiene & Safety

All tools are thoroughly sanitized between clients. No services will be performed on infected or damaged nails. Brush Tip Studio reserves the right to refuse service if necessary to protect client and technician health.

👧
Guests

No extra guests are permitted unless arranged beforehand. Our home studio is designed to be calm, focused, and comfortable for each individual client.

🏡
Home Salon Notice

Brush Tip Studio is a home-based salon. Pets may be present. Please inform us of any allergies beforehand so we can ensure a comfortable experience for you.

🔁
Repairs & Fixes

Free fixes within 3 days for workmanship-related issues only. No free repairs will be provided for client-caused damage. Please contact us promptly within this window.

📸
Social Media

Nail photos and videos may be taken for content purposes. Please let us know if you prefer not to be posted — we will always respect your privacy and never post without permission.

💬
Communication

Replies will be sent during business hours only. Please allow adequate time for responses. We appreciate your patience and will get back to you as soon as possible.
